Bangladesh Grading System

Official grade scale for SSC, HSC & University

Marks Range Letter Grade Grade Point Status
80 - 100 A+ 5.00 / 4.00 Outstanding
70 - 79 A 4.00 Excellent
60 - 69 A- 3.50 Very Good
50 - 59 B 3.00 Good
40 - 49 C 2.00 Satisfactory
33 - 39 D 1.00 Pass
0 - 32 F 0.00 Fail
4th Subject Bonus

If GPA in 4th subject > 2.00, bonus = (GPA - 2) added to total. Maximum GPA = 5.00

Fail Rule

Failing any main subject results in GPA 0.00
